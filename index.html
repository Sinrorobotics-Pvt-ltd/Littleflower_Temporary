    Our Mission

    To impart to the modern youth the technical trade knowledge and employability skills with dedication and commitment to enable them to make a positive difference in the world with their unique civic behavior

    Our Vision

    Little Flower Engineering Institute shall be known as the best centre of excellence imparting knowledge in technical trade and employability skills, to empower the modern youth with decent civic behavior to find their true potential and self-fulfillment, as they march ahead to fulfill their deep desire for their best contribution to their families, society and our motherland, while taking care of the interests of both the employees and the trustee.

    Welcome To
    Little Flower Engineering Institute

    More than five decades of didactic experience in the field of Industrial Technical Education in Kerala, particularly in our Archdiocese, has won many laurels. The envious performance of our Institute has set a new bench mark that has attracted students, teachers and recruitment agencies to be associated with us for quality and productive outcome. We deliver what we promise, as authenticated by stupendous responses from all quarters, particularly, the foreign recruiters.....

    With an expansive training space, housed in the picturesque piece of verdant land on the National Highway, is a bustling place, where overseasrecruitments take place on a daily basis. We have added various new facilities in our institute to augment par excellence the on-going training and testing needs of our students and national & international recruiting employers. We provide the post-matriculation students training, affiliated to the National Council of Vocational Training (NCVT), under the supervision of the Director General of Employment &Training, Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship, Government of India. Engineering Graduates and technically qualified diploma holders, join our specialized job-oriented courses (Advanced Training Programmes - ATP) for snatching quick employment opportunities abroad.Our experienced faculties transform the trainees into a new breed of employable & skilled employees with international competence. We feel blessed when our various associates vouch for the uniqueness of our Trade Testing Centre that is blended with training and testing - the only one such Institute in India. We invite you to visit us for your needs, for our eclectic institute is a compendium of experienced faculties, employable trainees and understanding employers.

    Transforming young lives was a dream of the Ernakulam Social Service Society of the Archdiocese of Verapoly in 1962 and was realized on a humble but firmly built foundation of a simple technical workshop in Kalamassery, named Little Flower Engineering Institute (LFEI) with St.Theresa of Lisieux, popularly known as "Little Flower" as its patron saint.
